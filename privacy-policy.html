Privacy Policy

App: CGDA
Last Updated: February 2026

Overview

CGDA is a fantasy football league app for the Chicagoland Gentlemen's Dynasty Association. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the app handles information.

Data We Do Not Collect

CGDA does not collect, store, or share any personal information. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not track your usage or behavior within the app.

Third-Party Data

CGDA displays fantasy football league data sourced from the Sleeper platform via their public API. We do not store or transmit any Sleeper data beyond what is displayed in the app. Please refer to Sleeper's privacy policy for information on how they handle your data.

Analytics & Tracking

CGDA does not use any analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

Children's Privacy

CGDA is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.
